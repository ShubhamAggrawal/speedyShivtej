Who
are we?
Launched in 2024, Our technology platform connects customers, restaurant partners and delivery partners, serving their multiple needs. We are Speedie, a passionate team dedicated to transforming food delivery in India. Our mission is to reach every village and small city, making delicious food accessible anytime, anywhere. By connecting local food vendors with customers through smart technology, we aim to serve with speed, reliability, and heart.
